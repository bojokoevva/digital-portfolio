HealthLink Diagnostics – Network Capstone Project

Design and security implementation of a multi-site healthcare network supporting EHR, PACS imaging, telehealth, and HIPAA-compliant data flow.

Network architecture Healthcare IT Cybersecurity HIPAA compliance
DATACOMM 4220 – Network Capstone
University of South Florida

Context & goal

HealthLink Diagnostics is a rapidly growing healthcare diagnostics company consolidating operations across multiple clinical sites into a centralized headquarters in Tampa, Florida. The organization required a secure, high-performance network capable of supporting electronic health records, medical imaging systems, and real-time clinical communication.

The goal of this capstone project was to design a scalable, secure, and HIPAA-compliant enterprise network connecting Tampa, Orlando, and Miami while ensuring reliability, redundancy, and performance under continuous growth.

Role Network architect & security designer
Setting Individual capstone project
Company size 180 employees with 15% annual growth
Budget $200,000 infrastructure investment

Network & infrastructure design

  • Designed 1 Gbps MPLS inter-office connectivity with redundant Internet access and automatic failover.
  • Implemented Layer 3 PoE+ switching for clinical devices and Wi-Fi 6 access points with healthcare-grade security.
  • Supported core medical platforms including EHR, PACS, HL7 messaging, and DICOM imaging.
  • Created role-based VLAN segmentation for lab, imaging, administration, and guest networks.

Security architecture

  • Deployed Next-Generation Firewalls (Palo Alto), Intrusion Prevention Systems (IPS), and Web Application Firewalls (WAF).
  • Implemented MFA, Active Directory & Azure AD for identity and access management.
  • Enforced TLS 1.3 encryption in transit and AES-256 encryption at rest.
  • Established Data Loss Prevention (DLP), email filtering, and secure mobile device management.

Implementation & operations

  • Four-week deployment across Tampa, Orlando, and Miami.
  • Redundant failover testing and performance baseline validation.
  • SIEM monitoring via Splunk with real-time alerting.
  • Defined RTO of 1 hour and RPO of 15 minutes for disaster recovery.

Business & technical impact

  • Enabled secure, real-time medical data exchange across sites.
  • Optimized large-file medical image transfers.
  • Delivered full HIPAA & HITECH compliance.
  • Designed infrastructure scalable for multi-year growth.
